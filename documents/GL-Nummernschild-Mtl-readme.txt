- About GL-Nummernschld-Mtl font
  GL-Nummernschld is German vehicle registration plates typeface font.
  This font has no small letters, and has characters of Adobe Western 2 and some more glyphs.
  We made this font refering to "StVO, FZV - Anlange 4", and get the typeface image from Wikipedia, and so on.
  And it has numberplate-like forms in unicode area E000 to E02C.
